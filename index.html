Meet UJPhotography

A storyteller
through a lens

Ujwal Johnson is a photographer from Port Coquitlam, British Columbia, Canada, whose gift lies in capturing emotion and weaving stories filled with magic and quiet dreams. He works as a part-time photographer and content creator, partnering with brands to bring their visions to life through strikingly beautiful visual narratives.

"To photograph is to hold a breath of the world, suspended in light."
