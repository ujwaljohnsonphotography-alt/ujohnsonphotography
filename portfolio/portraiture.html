Portfolio — People

Portraiture
& the human moment

Faces carry entire worlds. In this ongoing portrait series, I look for the quiet seconds between poses — the breath before a smile, the softness after a laugh — where a person's story speaks in light. Each frame is built with simple direction, natural gestures, and a palette that keeps attention where it belongs: on human presence.

01

Approach

Minimal setups, intentional light, and gentle prompts. I balance environment and expression to keep the photograph honest and cinematic at once.

02

Mood

Warm, textured, and calm. I favour skin-true tones and soft falloff to let eyes and micro-expressions carry the frame.
